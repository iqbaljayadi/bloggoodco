Popular Posts Bar WordPress Widget

Installation

  1. Unzip popularpostsbars.zip into your hard drive.
  2. Upload the whole popularpostsbars folder into your WP Plugins dir wp-content/plugins/
  3. Go to the Plugins section and activate the Popular Posts Bar Plugin.
  4. Head to Appearance » Widgets, drag and drop the Popular Posts Bar box into a Widget Area.
  5. Customize (Optional)
